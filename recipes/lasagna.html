Lasagna

Description of Lasagna

Lots of noodles, layered with cheese, meat and sauce blah blah blah.

Usually baked in the oven to melt the layers together.

Cottage cheese, cheddar, bolognaise and other stuff.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Bolognaise
  • Cheese
  • Cottage Cheese

Steps

  1. Get a pan, line with cheese
  2. Layer with noodles
  3. Layer noodles with bolognaise
  4. Layer with more noodles
  5. Another layer of cheese
  6. Bake at 425 for an hour
